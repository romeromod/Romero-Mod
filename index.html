Romero Mod

a total conversion mod for Left 4 Dead 2

The baying of the hordes in the distance always chills my heart. Am I out of ammunition? If I run, will I make it next door or just get swarmed? How can we move without getting separated? Oh, God... the tortured moans of the undead again. Lord, how I miss home.

About

Romero Mod is a total conversion mod for Left 4 Dead 2, inspired by George A. Romero's masterpiece zombie films.

The mod has the following goals:

  • Evoke a feeling of genuine dread
  • Improve game world immersion
  • Dramatically change the strategies required to win
  • Create something fun!

Installation

Place romeromod.vpk in the following folder (your path may vary slightly, depending on install location):

C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\left 4 dead 2\left4dead2\addons

Launch L4D2 and enjoy!

It is recommended that you disable all other L4D2 add-ons when using the Romero Mod. Conflicts will almost certainly occur when other mods are present!
